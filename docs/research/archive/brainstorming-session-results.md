### Insights & Learnings
**Key realizations from the session**

- **Psychological Positioning Beats Technical Accuracy:** AppSec leaders respond to "can't see my full attack surface" more than "343 vulnerabilities exist" - the former triggers budget conversations, the latter triggers fatigue

- **The Maturity Conversation is the Money Conversation:** When you reframe from "fix these bugs" to "your security infrastructure is architecturally insufficient for your application portfolio scale," you shift from tactical to strategic budget allocation

- **Show Don't Tell Creates Organic Wow:** Listing 41 apps, 6 stacks, 343 signals without saying "amazing scan!" lets the data volume speak for itself - more credible with technical buyers

- **The Attacker Journey is Universal:** Every AppSec leader understands recon â†’ target â†’ exploit. Structuring the report this way makes it intuitive and demonstrates "we think like your adversaries"
